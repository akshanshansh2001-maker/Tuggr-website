Who we are

Finding the right talent shouldn't feel like searching for a needle in a haystack.

At Tuggr, we partner with growing businesses to simplify recruitment through a structured, quality-first hiring approach. We don't believe recruitment ends when an offer is accepted — it begins by understanding your business, your culture, and the type of people who thrive within it.

Every search is approached with the mindset of becoming an extension of your internal hiring team. Whether you're hiring one strategic employee or building an entire business unit, our focus remains the same.

Quality over quantity.

Our philosophy

Most agencies optimize for submissions. We optimize for the pull that lasts.

Before presenting a candidate, we evaluate far more than a résumé. Our screening framework holds every candidate up against nine points of tension:

01Communication Skills
02Analytical Thinking
03Logical Reasoning
04Problem Solving
05Learning Agility
06Professional Behaviour
07Cultural Alignment
08Career Intentions
09Long-term Stability

A great hire isn't just someone who can do the job. It's someone who belongs in the team.
